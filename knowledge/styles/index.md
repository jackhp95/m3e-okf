# Styles

The expressive surface of the system: color, typography, shape, motion, elevation, and icons.

## Concepts

| Concept | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| [Color](/styles/color) | A role-based color system generated from a source color, cascading to every element. |
| [Elevation](/styles/elevation) | Hierarchy and layering expressed with tonal surface color plus shadow across six levels. |
| [Icons](/styles/icons) | Variable-font symbols controlled by four axes, kept to one style per screen. |
| [Motion](/styles/motion) | Consistent, emotionally tuned transitions driven by shared easing and duration. |
| [Shape](/styles/shape) | Corner rounding carries brand and hierarchy through a fixed radius scale. |
| [Typography](/styles/typography) | A tokenized type scale of named roles that encode size, weight, line height, and tracking. |
